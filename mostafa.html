What about the workshop

Join our CS workshop where experienced web developers will help students dive into coding, offering guidance on personal projects while exploring the exciting world of web development—participate to sharpen your skills, learn from the pros, and earn a Pizza Grant for your hard work! Whether you're a beginner or looking to level up, this is your chance to code, connect, and enjoy some delicious pizza along the way

Rules

to ensure a productive and enjoyable workshop, please follow these simple rules: stay quiet during the session to maintain focus and respect others, and use HTML and CSS to create a simple website as your project—no frameworks or advanced tools required! This approach will help you grasp the basics of web development while keeping things fun and accessible. Let’s keep the vibe collaborative, the code clean, and the pizza flowing!

What will you learn in this workshop?

In this workshop, you’ll learn the fundamentals of HTML and CSS, the building blocks of web development, and gain hands-on experience creating a simple website from scratch. Beyond that, you’ll be exposed to the exciting world of programming, discovering how coding works and how it powers the web. Whether you’re a complete beginner or looking to expand your skills, this session will give you a solid foundation and inspire you to explore more in the vast field of computer science. Get ready to unlock your coding potential!
